Weekly Diet Plan

Monday

Breakfast: Greek yogurt with berries and a sprinkle of granola.

Lunch Grilled chicken salad with mixed greens, cherry tomatoes, cucumbers, and balsamic vinaigrette.

Dinner: Baked salmon with quinoa and steamed broccoli.

Tuesday

Breakfast Whole grain toast with avocado and poached eggs.

Lunch: Turkey and avocado wrap with whole wheat tortilla, lettuce, and mustard.

Dinner: Stir-fried tofu with mixed vegetables (bell peppers, snap peas, carrots) served over brown rice.

Wednesday

Breakfast: Smoothie with spinach, banana, almond milk, and protein powder.

Lunch: Quinoa salad with chickpeas, diced bell peppers, cucumber, feta cheese, and lemon vinaigrette.

Dinner: Grilled shrimp skewers with roasted sweet potatoes and asparagus.

Thursday

Breakfast: Oatmeal topped with sliced bananas, almonds, and a drizzle of honey.

Lunch: Lentil soup with a side of whole grain bread.

Dinner: Grilled chicken breast with roasted Brussels sprouts and quinoa.

Friday

Breakfast: Scrambled eggs with spinach and whole grain toast

Lunch:Caprese salad with sliced tomatoes, fresh mozzarella, basil, and a drizzle of balsamic glaze.

Dinner: Whole wheat pasta with marinara sauce, lean ground turkey, and a side of steamed green beans.

Saturday

breakfast:Greek yogurt with berries and a sprinkle of granola.

lunch: Grilled chicken salad with mixed greens, cherry tomatoes, cucumbers, and a vinaigrette dressing.

dinner:Stir-fried tofu with mixed vegetables (bell peppers, snap peas, carrots) over brown rice.

Sunday

Breakfast:English breakfast, with orange juice

Lunch:Whole wheat wrap with hummus, spinach, grilled vegetables, and feta cheese.

Dinner:Turkey chili with kidney beans and diced vegetables.
